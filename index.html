Who Am I ?

Having experience more than 2 years in Software and Game Development, I am still pursuing my dreams as Game Developer and craving for higher education. My best tools are Unity3D using C# and Android Studio and sometimes I use Construct2 to test some ideas. I have experience in developing for iOS, Android, PC, and Mac platform.

I have essential knowledge in game development and project management. Being able to work in team and having good team management skills are my strong points. I like to grow a solid team to create a better work environment and lead it into developing better products.

Aside from my days as programmer, of course I love to play games. I mostly play story-rich JRPGs and strategy/tactics games. Final Fantasy Tactics Advance, The Legend of Heroes: Trails in The Sky, and Ys series are my favourites. No need to mention Dota 2 as the only e-sport game I have ever played. Apart from the computer, I love swimming and cycling every weekend.
